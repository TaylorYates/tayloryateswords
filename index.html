Who am I?

I'm a copywriter and journalist who has a seriously wide range of experience, and am, (hopefully?), just the person you're looking for.

My specialisation area is in music, arts and culture with a talent for interviewing, and have spoken to the likes of Kimbra, London Grammar, Lifehouse, Thelma Plum, Jenny Broke The Window and The Griswolds – to name few. However, I also have written: for a teen magazine; about what flowers you can use in tea; in the general hard news sector; covered and held an Editorial position in world news and much more. I've also recently branched out into Copywriting for web applications.

I'm also a Front End Web Developer and Web Designer – and yes, this means I know how to write about these topics as well.

Feel free to get in touch!

PORTFOLIO.

Birdee Magazine – Music Columnist.

For quite a long time, (I don't remember exactly how long, excuse me!), I was the music columnist at the now defunct Birdee Magazine. As it was a magazine for teenage girls, my language was always fun, cute and empowering – all while maintaining a solid amount of music cred. It was one of the most viewed and loved parts of the magazine and a lot of the followers still keep in touch with me today.
